Hazelnut · Search results redesign

A warmer, more modern results page

Replaces the flat black background and navy/blue selection state with a warm hazelnut-tinted dark theme, softer card surfaces, and an accent color that matches the app's identity. New typography too: Fraunces for headings and book titles, Manrope for everything else — a pairing with more personality than the default system font. What changed

  • Warm hazelnut background glow instead of flat black — matches the app's identity, still fully dark-mode.
  • Selection state is now a left accent stripe + warm tint instead of a blue ring, easier to scan at a glance.
  • Cards lift slightly on hover for a more tactile, modern feel.
  • Mobile gets a full-screen detail view with a sticky "Back to results" header, so nothing feels cramped on small screens.
  • Star ratings and pills use the same accent tint so the whole page reads as one coherent palette.
  • Results list now scrolls in its own panel (try it on the laptop preview) — the page and the detail panel stay put.
  • "What people say" only shows up once there's something to say — no empty heading when the LLM finds no reviews.
  • Fraunces + Manrope typography, applied consistently across the search results, detail, and home screens.
