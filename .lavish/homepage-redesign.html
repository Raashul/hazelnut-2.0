Specific issues to address (regardless of direction)

Critical

Idle state void

Replace the empty panel + dashed ghost box with a real hero — either centered search or curated content.

Critical

Search prominence

The search bar needs to be the first thing the eye lands on, not a small form tucked in the corner.

Important

Book card scannability

Covers need more visual weight. Rating and year should be styled as metadata chips, not plain text.

Important

Brand color

"Hazelnut" is a warm, tactile name. The all-gray palette misses it. Even a single amber accent would help.
